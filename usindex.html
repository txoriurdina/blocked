My story

I was born in Reims in France on November 17, 2007. But like my father is military, I often moved. After a year in my hometown, I moved for 2 years to Saint-Dizier where my little brother who is 3 years younger than me was born. Then we moved to Mont-de-Marsan for 6 years, that's where I grew up and it's the place I've always considered "the place I came from". Then, I moved for 1 year in Alabama in the United States. It was a big change but I was able to learn English almost fluently and I enjoyed it. After that, we went back to France for the capital, Paris, where we stayed for 4 years, from my 6th to 9th grade. I was also able to maintain my English thanks to advanced English courses. Finally. I arrived where I am now, in Bethesda, near Washington D.C., U.S.A, at the Lycée français de Rochambeau.
